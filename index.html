// What We Do

End-to-End
Assembly Services

From bare boards to fully tested assemblies. We handle the entire PCB assembly process so you can focus on building your product.

🔧

SMT Assembly

Surface mount technology placement and reflow for fine-pitch components. Precise, reliable, and repeatable results on every run.

🔩

THT Assembly

Through-hole component soldering for connectors, capacitors, and legacy components. Manual and wave solder capable.

🛠️

Prototype Runs

Starting from just 3 PCBs — perfect for R&D, validation, and proof-of-concept builds without committing to large volumes.

🔍

Inspection & Testing

Visual inspection and basic functional checks to catch defects before they reach your hands. Quality you can trust.

📦

Component Sourcing

Don't have parts? We can help source components locally or assist with BOM review to keep your project moving.

📐

Rework & Repair

Fix existing boards, replace components, and correct assembly errors — we rework what others can't.
